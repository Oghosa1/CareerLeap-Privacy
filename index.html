We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements across platforms. We do not receive your full payment card number from Apple, Google, or RevenueCat.

We use the information above to provide, maintain, and improve the Service; authenticate you; communicate about your account or the Service; comply with legal obligations; and protect the security and integrity of CareerLeap.

We work with service providers who process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards. Those providers are permitted to use such data only as needed to deliver the Service, consistent with their terms and our agreements. We do not sell your personal information.

For transparency, you should review the privacy notices of key subprocessors involved in the Service, including Apple (Apple Privacy Policy), Google (Google Privacy Policy), and RevenueCat (RevenueCat Privacy).

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information.
