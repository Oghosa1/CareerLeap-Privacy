Account & Data Deletion

CareerLeap is designed so you can understand and control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your account and the information we store for you.

How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion even if you have already removed the app from your device:

  1. Email guerrilladev@gmail.com with the subject line “CareerLeap — Account Deletion Request.”
  2. Send the message from, or clearly state, the email address associated with your CareerLeap account so we can verify ownership.
  3. After we confirm your request, we will delete your account and associated personal data within 7 business days, except where we must retain certain information to meet legal obligations (for example, limited billing records as required by law).

What We Delete

When your deletion request is completed, we aim to remove:

  • Your account credentials and profile information.
  • Content and activity stored in your account (for example, goals, preferences, and other materials you added in the app).
  • Historical usage tied to your account, where technically and legally feasible.

Subscriptions

Auto-renewable subscriptions are billed and managed by the store where you subscribed. Deleting your CareerLeap account does not automatically cancel an active subscription.

  • iOS (Apple App Store): Cancel in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, or follow Apple’s current support steps for managing subscriptions.
  • Android (Google Play): Open the Google Play Store app, go to your profile, then Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions, or use the subscription management options Google provides for your account.

For our full privacy practices, see the CareerLeap Privacy Policy.
